Link It

Link it is our first Android application and is leading the way in a social media sharing revolution! Link It allows you to experience next-generation social media sharing with a quick setup and a smooth and simple user experience.

Link It allows you to share that awesome article or update all of your social media at one time without having to open multiple apps! Link It removes the hassle and lets you share seamlessly to all of your friends and followers! Choose what you want to share and to whom and hit "Link It."

Link It allows you to sign into Facebook, Google+, and Twitter allowing you to share to each simultaneously. In addition to social media sites, Link It will also allow you to share links via SMS. In future updates we will be adding the ability to share images and be able to share through email.

Join the social media revolution today and start sharing! Click here to download.
